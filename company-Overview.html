Our story as ‘Cool Invoice’

A Trusted Solution by Ezzha Ltd

Cool Invoice is proudly a concern of Ezzha Ltd (www.ezzha.com), a company dedicated to transforming small business operations through innovation and automation. Since our journey began in 2019, Cool Invoice has been at the forefront of simplifying business processes bringing invoicing, inventory management, accounting, and automation under one seamless platform.

We believe in empowering small businesses to operate flawlessly and focus on growth while we handle the complexities with simplicity and excellence. Cool Invoice was established in 2019 with its initial members in Dunstable who shared one unified purpose to simplify business invoicing and financial administration for small organizations. Our team understood small business obstacles because we began as a single venture with big ambitions. Our mission to develop a technological invoicing system rests on creating a solution which operates as your business growth partner and not merely as a tool.

Our software platform addresses every fundamental requirement which small businesses need including flawless invoicing capabilities together with expense management tools alongside analytical reports and payment automation. Every business requires access to user-friendly powerful solutions which decrease stress while saving time and boosting efficiency. Throughout your business expansion Cool Invoice delivers technology solutions which enable you to concentrate on improving your business operations. Integrity directs all our actions while innovation powers our core organizational foundation and steady dedication to excellence enables us to serve your business growth. Your success is our mission.
